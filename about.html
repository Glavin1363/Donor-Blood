About DonorConnect

Connecting donors with recipients to save lives through innovative technology and compassionate community.

Our Mission

DonorConnect was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to bridge the gap between blood and organ donors and those in desperate need of these life-saving resources.

In India, thousands of patients die each year due to the unavailability of compatible donors. Our platform leverages technology to create a nationwide network of verified donors, making the matching process faster, more efficient, and more accessible to everyone.

We believe that no one should lose their life because they couldn't find a donor in time. By connecting donors and recipients through our secure platform, we're creating a community that saves lives every day.
